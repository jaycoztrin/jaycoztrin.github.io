1. How do I pay Jayleigh my robux for paid commissions?

    There are two ways to donate robux, you can either buy a t-shirt made by me, or you can pay through group funds. Tax will apply when you pay through a t-shirt, but not when you pay through a group.
  2. Can Jayleigh do everything?

    No. Jayleigh can not do everything. She is still learning, and she is trying to do everything to the best of her abilities.
  3. Why is Jayleigh taking so long on my commission?

    Jayleigh is very busy with work and school even in the summer, so please be patient and Jayleigh will get to your commission soon!
  4. Why can't I order more than one commission at a time?

    The reason why you can’t order more than one commission at a time is so Jayleigh doesn’t get too overwhelmed with your commissions. Jayleigh would like to get to everyone and not just one person.
  5. Why do I need to pay extra robux when I pay for my commission?

    Unfortunately, Roblox takes a “tax” on any sold item, for t-shirts it’s 30% of the final price. So for Jayleigh to receive the correct amount of robux and counteract this tax more robux is required.
